Our Mission:

The Green Thumb Garden Club is committed to fostering a love for gardening and environmental stewardship. We aim to create beautiful, sustainable gardens and educate the community on eco-friendly practices.

Our History:

Founded in 2010, The Green Thumb Garden Club started with a small group of friends who shared a love for gardening. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of over 100 members, hosting numerous events and workshops.
